# 🏍️ Wheelie Good Weather
# Because checking five different weather apps is a workout you don't need.

## The Problem
If you ride, you know the drill: You wake up, check the weather. It looks gray. You check a different app. Maybe a third. You're looking for that tiny, glorious window of dry pavement just so you can justify putting your gear on. It’s a mental tax every rider pays.

## The Solution
Wheelie Good Weather automates the hopium. It aggregates data from multiple weather APIs to give you a definitive verdict: Is it a riding day, or is the bike staying in the garage?

## 🛠️ Key Features
Multi-Source Consensus: Aggregates data from various APIs to ensure you aren't being lied to by a single source.

"The Verdict": A simple, glanceable status—Wheelie Good or Garage Day.

Smart Notifications: Instead of you hunting for info, the app pings you when a riding window opens up.

Custom Thresholds: (Planned) Define what "good weather" means to you. Is 10% rain okay? Are you a "no-go" under 10°C?
